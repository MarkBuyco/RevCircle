Community Guidelines

Be respectful. Be helpful. Be passionate about cars.

⬅ Back to Homepage

📌 General Rules

  • Respect all members—no bullying, hate speech, or harassment.
  • No spam, ads, or self-promotion outside of allowed areas.
  • Keep language friendly and appropriate for all ages.

🛠️ Posting

  • Write clear, descriptive titles.
  • Explain your post with context, questions, or story.
  • Share relevant images if available, and avoid spreading misinformation.

💬 Commenting

  • Stay on-topic and contribute meaningfully.
  • Be constructive—not rude or sarcastic.
  • Support others’ ideas and share your perspective respectfully.

🚫 Violations

  • Rule-breaking may lead to deleted posts, warnings, or bans.
  • Repeated or extreme behavior can lead to permanent removal.
